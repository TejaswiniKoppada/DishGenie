DishGenie - Help

1. DishGenie

DishGenie is an AI-powered recipe recommendation and pantry management system. It predicts recipes based on user-selected ingredients using deep learning and helps organize pantry items efficiently.

2. Login / Signup

Register with your name, email, and password to create an account. Your credentials are securely stored, allowing seamless access to your personalized cooking experience.

3. Pantry Tracker

Add ingredients via text or image upload. The system verifies them against the dataset and updates your pantry. Keep track of what you have and avoid food waste!

4. Selecting Ingredients

Choose available ingredients from a searchable multi-select list. Select your desired items and proceed to generate recipes tailored to your pantry.

5. Generating recipes

Our AI predicts the best recipes based on your selected ingredients. View details, including cuisine type, prep time, and nutritional breakdown.

6. Saving Your Favorite Recipes with Download

Save your favorite recipes for later access. Download recipes as PDFs or revisit them in the saved recipes section, with the option to delete when needed.

7. Surprise recipe

Feeling adventurous? Get a random AI-generated recipe, regardless of your pantry items, and try something new and exciting!
